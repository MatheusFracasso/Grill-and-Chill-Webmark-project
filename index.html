“Discover the best BBQ spots in Noorderpark”

Welcome to Grill & Chill Noorderpark!

The website is your great resource when it talks about barbecue in Amsterdam Noord. In case you’re planning a barbecue with friends in a peaceful picnic in nature, you must know that the Geemente of Amsterdam has only allowed a few public parks for barbecuing.

In the case of Amsterdam Noord, only certain areas of Noorderpark are permitted, but don't worry, we will provide you with the details on which areas are allowed and how to find them.

What is the purpose of the website?

The main purpose of “Grill & Chill Noorderpark” is to be your ultimate guide for a great barbecue time in Noorderpark. We are working to guarantee the best information about available locations, facilities, restrictions, and good tips for a good BBQ time.

For who is this website?

This website is designed for anyone who loves spending time outdoors—whether you're a local, a tourist, or simply looking to organize a fun barbecue or picnic with friends and family.

Explore one of Amsterdam's most vibrant parks and follow our website to learn the correct spots and tips to make your “grill” time “chill,” memorable, and within the rules.
